Bio

Movement that is transformative, joyful, and deeply restorative.

With an extensive background in dance and fitness, Renay has dedicated her career to inspiring movement that is both transformative and joyful. Pilates, barre, and rebounder training are among her specialties, and her classes are known for being thoughtfully challenging, energizing, and filled with laughter.

As a certified Somatic Breathwork facilitator, Renay combines mindful movement with nervous system regulation, creating experiences that help people reduce stress, restore balance, and reconnect with themselves on a deeper level. Her holistic approach encourages greater openness of the body, mind, heart, and spirit, leaving participants feeling renewed from the inside out.

Renay is the co-creator and lead instructor of Yacht Pilates, an innovative waterfront wellness experience in Marina del Rey that is redefining what self-care can look like. Created in partnership with Aloni Ford, Yacht Pilates blends Pilates, somatic breathwork, sound healing, and curated wellness activations into an unforgettable mini retreat on the water.

More than a fitness class, Yacht Pilates is a celebration of movement, community, and intentional living, bringing together wellness experts, meaningful connections, and the healing power of the ocean in one inspiring experience.

Throughout the summer, Renay and Aloni continue to expand Yacht Pilates with immersive events that make luxury wellness approachable, uplifting, and accessible to all. Together, they are building a vibrant community where people can move, restore, connect, and leave feeling empowered.

When she is not leading Yacht Pilates experiences, Renay teaches group reformer Pilates at Mighty Pilates in Santa Monica and offers private in-home sessions tailored to clients seeking personalized, results-driven movement and wellness coaching.
