Welcome Aboard

Welcome to Yacht Pilates

At Yacht Pilates, we believe wellness should be beautiful, inspiring, and shared. Set aboard a stunning 130-foot classic yacht in the heart of Marina del Rey, our signature experiences are more than a Pilates class. They are mini waterfront retreats designed to restore your body, calm your mind, and nourish your soul.

Every event blends movement, mindfulness, and luxury with curated spa-inspired wellness experiences including Pilates, sound baths, somatic breathwork, massages, mini facials, cold plunges, wellness activations, healthy bites, fresh juices, live music, and thoughtful surprises. Surrounded by the healing energy of the ocean, you will leave feeling stronger, lighter, and deeply recharged.

But what truly makes Yacht Pilates special is the community. We have created a welcoming space where strangers become friends, conversations happen naturally, and meaningful connections are made. Whether you are coming solo or with a friend, you will be embraced by a community that celebrates wellness, adventure, and living life fully.

Because we believe the best kind of luxury is not just found in beautiful places. It is found in how you feel when you leave.

Move. Restore. Connect. Welcome aboard.
