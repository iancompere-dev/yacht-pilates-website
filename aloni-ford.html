Bio

Creating unforgettable experiences that inspire people to move, connect, and thrive.

With a career spanning entertainment, sports, and community leadership, Aloni has built a reputation for creating unforgettable experiences that inspire people to move, connect, and thrive.

For over 17 years, Aloni has managed professional NBA players, combining strategic leadership with a passion for bringing people together. Her background in entertainment and event production has led to collaborations with world-class talent, brands, and organizations, allowing her to create experiences that are both elevated and deeply intentional.

A lifelong advocate for expanding access to boating and waterfront experiences, Aloni serves as Vice Commodore of the Women's Sailing Association of Santa Monica Bay and is an Ambassador for the California Yacht Club. She is also the founder of the Yacht Girls Bookclub, an innovative community that has been featured in the Los Angeles Times, Spectrum News, and numerous magazine publications for redefining how women gather, connect, and build community.

Through Yacht Pilates, Aloni is reimagining wellness by blending movement, mindfulness, luxury, and nature into unforgettable waterfront experiences. Her vision is simple: create spaces where people feel welcome, leave inspired, and discover that some of life's most meaningful moments happen on the water.

“Wellness is not just about movement, it is about creating experiences that reconnect us with ourselves, each other, and the beauty around us.”

Aloni Ford, Co-Founder of Yacht Pilates
